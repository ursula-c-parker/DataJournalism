About our project

We each came up with our own design for the macro and microviews, and then took the best elements of each model and combined them into a unified approach that is understandable, simple, and aesthetically pleasing. We chose to use materialize as our CSS platform.

  • Life expectancy refers to average age at which a person dies in the given region.
  • Infant mortality refers to the rate at which infants do not survive past their first birthday.
  • Data is presented as an average rate per 1000 people in a given region.








    • Overall, we took the best design principles from both microviews and macroviews. At the end of the day, we decided to focus only on life expectancy because adding different causes of death made the site seem too chaotic. We kept our build pipeline intact and utilized d3 to help us with visualization.

    We are doing everything 50/50 with Malachy working on the build pipeline and project organization, and Ursula working on data cleansing and creating the microviews/macroview.
